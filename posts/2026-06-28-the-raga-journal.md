**Indian Classical Music**

**​Indian classical music is a profound and ancient art form with roots dating back thousands of years. It is broadly divided into two major traditions: Hindustani music from the north and Carnatic music from the south. The foundational pillars of this musical system are Raga (the melodic framework) and Tala (the rhythmic cycle).**
